Albert Rodríguez-Sala



PhD candidate in Economics at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) and Barcelona School of Economics (BSE).


My research lies in the field of growth and development. In particular, I study how risk and risk-dealing mechanisms, as income smoothing or informal social insurance, interlink with economic growth and welfare. To do so, I primarily work with heterogeneous-agent models and household survey data from African countries.

I am on the 2021/2022 job market and I will be available for interviews at the EJME and ASSA Virtual Meetings. I will present my work at the Symposium of the Spanish Economic Association (SAEe).


Research Interests

Growth and Development, Macroeconomics and Inequality.
